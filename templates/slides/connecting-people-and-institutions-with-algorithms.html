Connecting People and Institutions with Algorithms 5/24

Connecting People

  • to their own and external institutions

    Institutional information and reporting requirements for libraries takes significant effort (at least at a small institution like Colorado College), These requirements are primary source of "pull" for the Catalog Pull Platform.

  • to algorithms that impact their work & their lives

    With Marc Andreessen's assertion that "Software is Eating The World", the more we can open-up our systems for algorithmic access, specifically APIs, the more utility we give to our communities. At the minimum, an API should be one of first requirements for new or updating existing systems.

  • and most importantly to other People

    Our systems are a two-way conversation between the institution and the end users and patrons. Understanding our patrons and our communities comes the most significant source of pull in the Catalog Pull Platform.
