Traditional Push Processes

TL&DR - "We already know what you want and this is our product pitch"

What do we mean by push?

With it's origins in early modern industrial companies, best epitomized by Ford Motor Company, Push is about the ability of large companies and organizations to accurately predict and mold demand of customers and organize their operations around those predictions.

Characteristics:

  • Top down design
  • Centralized control
  • Procedural & Tightly coupled
  • Marketing-focused
