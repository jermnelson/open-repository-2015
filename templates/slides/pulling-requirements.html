Pulling Requirements

Inspired by both the Lean Startup and Toyota's Lean Manufacturing, the Catalog Pull Platform moves away from trying to anticipate the needs and then "pushing" services and technology to patrons and staff, but instead identifies and responds to needs and demands for library technology by "pulling" directly from various constituencies served by libraries and cultural heritage organizations.

Pull models are emerging as a response to growing uncertainty. Instead of dealing with uncertainty through tighter control, pull models do the opposite. They seek to expand the opportunity for creativity by local participants dealing with immediate needs…Rather than seeking to constrain the resources available to people, pull models strive to continually expand the choices available while at the same time helping people to find the resources that are most relevant to them.
John Seely Brown and John Hagel III, 2008 in From Push To Pull : Emerging Models For Mobilizing Resources
